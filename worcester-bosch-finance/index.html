Boiler Finance

New Boiler Finance — Spread the Cost

Don't let the upfront cost of a new boiler put you off. A.M. Lander offers flexible finance options so you can spread the cost of a new Worcester Bosch boiler installation — with 0% interest options available.

Finance options for a new boiler

A new boiler is a significant investment — but it doesn't have to mean a large upfront payment. Through our finance partner TradeHelp, A.M. Lander can offer flexible payment plans to help you spread the cost over a period that suits you.

How does boiler finance work?

The process is straightforward:

  • Andy visits your home and provides a free, fixed-price installation quote
  • If you choose to proceed with finance, you complete a quick credit application through TradeHelp
  • Subject to approval, you choose your repayment period
  • Andy installs your new boiler — work begins only after finance is confirmed
  • You make monthly payments directly to the finance provider

Worcester Bosch Accredited Installer

As a Worcester Bosch Accredited Installer, Andy can offer extended warranties of up to 12 years on qualifying Worcester Bosch boiler installations — significantly longer than the standard warranty available from non-accredited installers. This extended warranty is included in your fixed installation price.

What boilers are available on finance?

Finance is available on all new boiler installations carried out by A.M. Lander, including:

  • Worcester Bosch Greenstar combi boilers
  • Worcester Bosch Greenstar system boilers
  • Worcester Bosch Greenstar regular boilers
  • Vaillant ecoTEC combi, system and regular boilers
  • ATAG high-efficiency boilers
  • All other makes Andy installs

Is finance right for me?

Finance is subject to status and credit check. Andy can talk you through the options when he visits for your free quote — there is no obligation to apply and no pressure to proceed. The credit check is carried out by TradeHelp and their lending partner Novuna Personal Finance.
