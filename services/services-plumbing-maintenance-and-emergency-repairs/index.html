Plumbing Services

Reliable plumbing — honest pricing

Whether it's a dripping tap you've been meaning to fix for months or a burst pipe at 10pm on a Sunday, Andy provides fast, professional plumbing services with no hidden charges and no upselling.

With over 30 years of experience across domestic and commercial properties, there's very little Andy hasn't seen and fixed.

Emergency plumbing:

  • Burst pipes — locate, isolate and repair
  • Leaking boilers and cylinders
  • Major leaks and flood damage containment
  • Frozen and blocked pipes
  • Failed stop cocks and isolation valves
  • Overflowing tanks and cisterns

Routine plumbing maintenance:

  • Leaking taps — washers, cartridges, replacement
  • Toilets — cisterns, flush valves, seats, blockages
  • Radiator problems — cold spots, leaks, balancing
  • Shower and bath plumbing repairs
  • Kitchen sink and waste plumbing
  • Dishwasher and washing machine connections
  • Expansion vessel and pressure issues
  • Thermostatic radiator valves (TRVs)
  • Motorised valves and zone controls
  • Pump replacement and system flushing
