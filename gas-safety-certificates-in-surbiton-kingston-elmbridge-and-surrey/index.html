Gas Safety Certificates (CP12)

Legally compliant gas safety inspections and CP12 certificates for landlords and homeowners across Surbiton, Kingston, Elmbridge, Esher and throughout Surrey and SW London.

What's Included

Fast, compliant CP12 inspections — done properly

Andy carries out thorough gas safety inspections to the standards required by the Health and Safety Executive. Every inspection is completed by a fully Gas Safe registered engineer (Reg. No. 199048), and your CP12 certificate is issued the same day.

⚠️ Landlord legal obligation

The Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations 1998 require landlords to have all gas appliances and flues inspected annually by a Gas Safe registered engineer and to provide tenants with a copy of the CP12 certificate. Failure to comply is a criminal offence.

What the inspection covers:

  • Checking all gas appliances are working safely and correctly
  • Inspecting all gas pipework and connections for leaks
  • Checking ventilation is adequate for all appliances
  • Inspecting flues and chimneys for safe operation
  • Verifying safe operating pressures and gas rates
  • Checking for carbon monoxide risks
  • Testing safety devices and shut-offs
  • Issuing the CP12 Landlord Gas Safety Record on completion
