About A.M. Lander

30 Years in the Making — Meet Andy

Andy Lander has been keeping homes warm and safe across Surrey and South West London since the early 1990s. What started with British Gas and Worcester Bosch became a trusted local business built entirely on reputation.

From British Gas to your front door

Andy started his career with British Gas, where he built the technical foundations and diagnostic skills that still define his work today. He went on to become an accredited Worcester Bosch engineer — gaining deep, manufacturer-level knowledge of one of the UK’s leading boiler brands.

In the early 1990s, Andy made the decision to go independent and start A.M. Lander — not to grow a large company, but to do the job he loves to the standard he believes in, for the local community he knows well.

Thirty-plus years later, the business is still just Andy. Every job is carried out by him personally — no subcontractors, no agency engineers. When you book with A.M. Lander, you get Andy.
