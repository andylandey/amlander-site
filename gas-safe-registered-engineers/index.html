Gas Services

Gas Safe Registered Engineers

With over 30 years of experience, Andy provides expert gas installations, servicing, repairs and safety inspections across Surrey and South West London. Fully Gas Safe registered (Reg. 199048) — no shortcuts, no exceptions.

What We Do

Expert gas work — done right, done safely

As a Gas Safe registered engineer with over 30 years of hands-on experience — including years with British Gas and Worcester Bosch — Andy has the knowledge and qualifications to handle any gas job, large or small.

Every job is carried out to the highest safety standards, with upfront pricing and honest advice. No upselling, no jargon.

Services include:

  • Boiler installation, replacement and commissioning
  • Annual boiler servicing across all makes and models
  • Boiler repairs and fault diagnosis
  • Warm air unit installation, service and repair
  • Gas fire installation and servicing
  • Gas meter works and pipework
  • Gas cooker and appliance installation
  • Landlord and homeowner gas safety inspections (CP12)
  • Pre-purchase property gas inspections
  • Carbon monoxide and gas leak investigations
  • Central heating system installation and upgrades
  • Pressure testing and gas tightness checks

Accredited installer for:

As a Worcester Bosch Accredited Installer, Andy can offer extended warranties of up to 12 years on qualifying new boiler installations.
