Common Questions

What you might want to know

How quickly can Andy come out?

Andy offers same-day callouts for emergencies across Surrey and SW London. For non-urgent work, appointments are usually available within a few days. Call 020 3355 0731 to check availability.

Do you charge for quotes?

Free estimates and quotations are provided for installation and remedial repair work, following a pre-booked chargeable diagnosis visit or survey. Emergency call-outs are charged at an hourly rate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, bank transfer, and cash. Finance options are also available through our partner TradeHelp — ask Andy for details.

Are you Gas Safe registered?

Yes — Andy is fully Gas Safe registered (Reg. No. 199048). You can verify this on the Gas Safe Register website. Every gas job is carried out legally, safely and to the highest standard.

Do you offer emergency plumbing?

Yes. Andy offers emergency callouts for plumbing and heating problems outside of normal hours. Call 020 3355 0731 and Andy will do his best to attend as quickly as possible.

Is your work guaranteed?

All installations and repairs come with a full workmanship guarantee. As a qualified gas trainer, mentor and assessor, Andy holds every job to the highest professional standard.

Need a boiler quote?

Get a free, no-obligation boiler quote. Andy will assess your home and give you a clear price — no hidden costs.
